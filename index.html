About This Documentation

This documentation hub serves as a comprehensive knowledge transfer and handoff resource for the AMD Ethernet Design Hub. It contains essential information, assets, and resources created and curated by AJ Dahal (Sr. Product Application Engineer - WTS-WWG).

📦

Included Resources

Reference designs, debugging playbooks, Answer Records (ARs), device tree configurations, driver patches, Jenkins automation workflows, and comprehensive testing documentation.

🎯

Purpose

Ensure seamless continuity of Ethernet-related projects, provide quick access to critical resources, and maintain institutional knowledge for future team members.
